About me

Behzad Javadian – Integration and Migration Educationalist

With a robust background in journalism from Iran and extensive experience as an integration and migration educationalist in Sweden, I bring a unique perspective to the field of cultural integration. My journey has endowed me with well-developed communication skills and the ability to navigate and facilitate both motivating and difficult conversations.

Living in Sweden has allowed me to gain a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities faced by refugees and immigrants. My work has honed my expertise in recognizing and addressing the diverse backgrounds of individuals, ensuring that each person’s unique story is acknowledged and respected.

My knowledge of multicultural Sweden and the dynamics of cultural meetings empowers me to effectively guide individuals and communities towards successful integration. Whether you're seeking advice on navigating the complexities of Swedish society or looking for strategies to foster a more inclusive environment, I am here to assist.

Feel free to contact me to find the best path to integration in Swedish society. Together, we can create a more inclusive and understanding community.
